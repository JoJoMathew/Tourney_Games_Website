The Beginnings

By James Hapson

Itâ€™s crazy to think the idea of Tourney Games began before the invention of the Smartphone, the reality is the original idea was developed when I was 16 years old. Consoles had been out for sometime but for me PC was king and that is what the intended market was for Tourney Games.

I came across a very popular multiplayer game online, Miniclip Pool. This was in the days when you could create your own table and others players from around the globe could compete against you for points. As I got better I thought it would be nice to get rewarded for my play with real world rewards.

After months of researching and finding out how to create a gambling site where the house never wins. I soon started talking to experts and the gambling commission about how I could develop such a site. At that time the technology for the vision just wasnâ€™t strong enough and the time jsut didn't feel right for me so Tourney Games was put on the back burner, but never out of mind.

During the down period of Tourney Games I was developing Card Games and Board games to license to Hasbro. This is still a very small part of what we do, but something fascinating happened...

Along came the Smartphone era. The App Stores started to take off and provided a platform for delivery. Technology had come a long way since the original inception of the Tourney Games concept and this got me thinking about the original idea again.

The journey of Tourney Games really took off with a new job where I met a developer, Mathew Thomas. After we had been speaking for a few weeks he soon introduced me to Marek Sieczko, a close friend who had a history of developing mobile games.

We soon organised a meeting a decided to form a partnership. Since then we have been working hard towards the release of our first App. Our goal is to become the go-to platform for competetive play and rewards.

Stay tuned!
