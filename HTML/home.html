Bounce Along

A procedurally-generated, infinite runner in development for iOS & Android. Bounce Along has you bouncing a ball through a randomly-generated level where no two levels are ever the same.

The game will integrate competitive E-Sport style game mechanics with live scoreboards and global rankings as well as daily and weekly competitions. Players will be able to win prizes which will at first be mainly digital rewards.

New balls, rosettes to show on your player card and Tourney Coins (the games currency) will be awarded but as the platform grows we will look to award cash prizes redeemable through this website based on your ranking on the global leaderboard

Bounce along will focus on a level playing field and moves away from the pay-to-win model found in many games. The most skilful players will always win.

As development progresses we will upload regular blog posts and videos detailing the updates.
